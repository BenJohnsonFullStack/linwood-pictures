Linwood Pictures

A Film & Television Production Company

We All Have a Story

Here at Linwood Pictures, we believe in the power of telling compelling stories that reflect the diverse perspectives and interests of those who tell them. If a picture is worth a thousand words, then a moving picture might be worth a million. We foster, develop and produce narrative features, television, documentaries, and animated content that bring creative and vibrant stories to a viewing audience, because nothing moves us the way the art of visual motion moves us.

Jacy Jenkins
Head of Marketing & Finance

A pioneer of the film community in Columbus, GA, Jacy co-founded Way Down Film Festival and was recruited by Fun Academy Motion Pictures as VP of Partnerships & Outreach in 2016, and founded Pop Up Video Film Festival in 2019. A consultant in entertainment media, Jacy is working to keep the dream of international film and animation alive everywhere.

Ben Johnson
Head of Development

A producer of independent film and television since 2016, Ben is dedicated to bringing unique and compelling artist stories to life on screen. With a focus on development, and a philosophy that all good stories start with the script, Ben aims to bring diverse voices to the forefront of independent film and television production for years to come.
